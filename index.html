LOMÉ CAFÉ

LOMÉ cafe brings the Authentic Energy and Social culture of West Africa through traditional foods, service and collaborations. Our concept combines variability, ambiance, entertainment and a superior staff to produce a sense of comfort and inspiration, all while highlighting the value of the African Experience.

LOMÉ achieves this goal by:

[1] Offering quality food at reasonable prices

[2] Designing and utilizing our space to welcome millennials and creative groups and by

[3] Actively considering the comfort of its customers and staff to maximize their social experience.
